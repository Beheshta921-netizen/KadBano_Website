About Us

KadBano Restaurant, located in the heart of Badakhshan, Afghanistan, is a place where tradition, taste, and warmth come together. Founded and owned by Semin Karim, a talented and dedicated woman, KadBano represents passion, resilience, and excellence in Afghan cuisine. With a strong commitment to quality and hospitality, the restaurant offers delicious traditional dishes prepared with care, fresh ingredients, and authentic flavors.
